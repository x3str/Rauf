Boeing

Many airlines choose us

Boeing, founded in 1916 by William Boeing in Seattle, Washington, is a leading global aerospace manufacturer. Initially starting with seaplanes, Boeing quickly expanded its product line, playing a crucial role in aviation history. During World War II, Boeing produced numerous military aircraft that significantly aided the war effort. In the post-war era, Boeing transformed commercial aviation with the introduction of the 707 jetliner. Over the years, Boeing has continued to innovate, developing iconic aircraft such as the 737, 747, 777, and the 787 Dreamliner. Today, Boeing remains at the forefront of aerospace technology, contributing to commercial aviation, defense, space exploration, and related services.
